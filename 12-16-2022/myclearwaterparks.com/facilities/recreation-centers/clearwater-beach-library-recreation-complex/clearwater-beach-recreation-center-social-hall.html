Clearwater Beach Social Hall

Font Size: + -

With spectacular waterfront views of the intercoastal and close proximity to the beach, the Clearwater Beach Social Hall is the perfect venue to host your next event, wedding reception or ceremony.

The 3, 243-sq.-foot social hall has 11 foot ceilings, can host up to 217 people standing or 180 for a seated dinner and includes a food preparation area, additional 625-sq. foot multipurpose room, scenic viewing deck and waterfront park.

69 Bay Esplanade ~ Clearwater, FL 33767
Phone: (727) 462-6138

What is included in my rental?

We want the set-up and breakdown of your event to be as seamless as possible, so we provide a multitude of on-site amenities such as 190 white padded folding chairs, 18 five-foot round banquet tables (8 guests) and 18 six-foot rectangular banquet tables. A on-site staff member during the entirety of your event as well as staff to help set up and break down the tables and chairs is also included in your rental. The food preparation area includes a pass-through window, ample countertop space, two large refrigerators and a microwave.

Do you have availability?
Yes, we have availability for events in 2022 and 2023.
How much does it cost to rent?

The Clearwater Beach Social Hall is one of the most affordable event venues on Clearwater Beach. The pricing structure includes a base fee and an hourly rate for every hour you are in the facility (including setting up, caterers, decorators, etc.).

Friday-Sunday Base Fee: $700

Monday-Thursday: $250

Hourly Rate: $50

Holiday Hourly Rate: $75

Waterfront Park Add-On: $100 per hour

There is also 10% non-resident fee for those who live outside the city of Clearwater and 7 percent state sales tax.  Pricing includes the use of the Social Hall, Kitchen, and the Multi-Purpose Room. Adding time to your rental agreement with less than 14 days notice would be pending staff availability and would be at the overtime rate of $75 per hour if approved.

The down payment will be forfeited if for any reason the event date is canceled or changed.  If the user cancels or transfers their building rental 14 or more days prior to their event, they are entitled to a refund of any money paid over the required down payment plus tax.  If the user cancels or transfers their building rental less than 14 days prior to their event, no refund will be given.

*The city will not be responsible for items, which are lost, stolen, or damaged before, during or after your event.

When can I rent the venue?

The Clearwater Beach Social Hall is available to rent on Friday nights, Saturdays, and Sundays and end by 1 a.m. Weekdays are available at the discretion of staff.

How close are you to the beach?
Whether you are looking to have your ceremony on the beach or just take photos, the social hall is only a 4 minute walk from the nearest Clearwater Beach access point.
Do you allow outside caterers?
Yes, you can use the catering company of your choice. We also provide a food preparation area with a pass-through window, ample countertop space, two large refrigerators and a microwave.
Can I bring my own alcohol?

Insurance and permits: If alcohol is being served at no charge, the User or a vendor hired by the User will need to provide an insurance policy including General Liability and Host Liquor Liability with the limits of at least $1,000,000 each occurrence/$2,000,000 general aggregate. They will need to list the City of Clearwater as additional insured. COI is due 14 days prior to the event. Without valid COI, alcohol will not be allowed to be served at the event. If the User is using a vendor to handle alcoholic beverage sales to the attendees, in addition to the above insurance requirements, the vendor must provide a permit to sell alcohol. If the User desires to sell alcoholic beverages without the use of a licensed vendor, a ONE-DAY NON-PROFIT CIVIC PERMIT shall be obtained from the Division of Alcoholic Beverages and Tobacco Department (located at 1313 N. Tampa St, Tampa, phone # (813) 272-2610). User shall supply proof of permit to the City 14 days prior to the event. Permission to sell alcohol must be obtained prior to the event.

Alcohol is not permitted when the building is open to the public during the daytime or in the waterfront park. 

Do you require us to use a specific list of vendors?
No, we do not have preferred or required vendors.
Where can my guests park?

Parking is not included with your rental. There are 62 pay-by-plate parking spaces located near the recreation center. They are on a first-come, first-serve basis, unless you contact the City of Clearwater Parking Division at (727) 562-4704 to pay to reserve spaces. There are 21 free parking spaces dedicated for recreation complex patrons and those with resident parking permits, but they cannot be reserved and you must obtain a permit upon arrival from staff. 

Are there any restrictions?
  • Smoking is not permitted inside of the building.
  • Users must follow the City’s noise ordinance and cannot have amplified sound outside after 11 p.m.
  • Any fabric decorations must be fire rated.
  • No tape or adhesive allowed on walls or floors.
  • No glitter allowed.
  • No artificial petals on ground in the building or park.
How do I reserve my date?
Complete the Recreation Facility Agreement with a staff member during business hours Monday - Friday.  Reservations require a down payment equal to the base fee (see above pricing).  Payments can be made by checks drawn on a US bank, or Visa and Master Card credit cards.  The balance is due 14 days prior to the event date.  At the time the balance is paid, you will need to fill out one of our Social Hall Diagrams with your desired set up.
How do I get there?

Take State Road 60 west over the Memorial Causeway to the round-a-bout on Clearwater Beach.  Take the North Beach exit from the round-a-bout going north on Mandalay Ave. Turn right on Bay Esplanade.  The Social Hall is at the end of the block on the right.
