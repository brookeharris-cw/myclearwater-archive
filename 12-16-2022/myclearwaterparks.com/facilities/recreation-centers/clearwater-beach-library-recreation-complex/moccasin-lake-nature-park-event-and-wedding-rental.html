Moccasin Lake Nature Park Event and Wedding Rental

With scenic oak-line paths and lakeside views, a variety of amenities and up-to-date technology, we promise to make your celebration one to remember.

Moccasin Lake Nature Park • 2750 Park Trail Ln. • 727-562-4315, ext. 1.

Rental Spaces

Garden Lodge

This woodsy lodge looks like it was picked right off a Vermont mountain and dropped into the lush 51-acre nature preserve. Inside, you’ll find a spacious garden lodge that can host up to 90 people standing or 68 people seated. The lodge has a gorgeous view of the butterfly and pollinator gardens and is located right next to the turtle pond. It is also equipped with the latest audio and video technology.

Picnic Pavilion

The large, covered pavilion is located next to the Birds of Prey exhibit and is equipped with rows of picnic tables and two propane grills. It can seat up to 50 people and is lighted at night. 

Wooded Amphitheater

Located by Moccasin Pond, the amphitheater provides a unique setting in the woods for a ceremony or presentation. The pews can sit up to 60 people and radiate in a semicircle from the center. 

Upper Meadow

The upper meadow is an open, wide expansive field that can be used for several scenarios such as large ceremony or a tented reception or event space. You and your guests will be near nature with the bird blind and butterfly and pollinator garden adjacent to the festivities. 

Moccasin Lake Stage

The Moccasin Lake Stage is located near the entrance to the park. The covered stage is set on a slope in an open lawn coupled by Magnolia trees. The stage can act as an arch or a platform.

Nature Center Patio

The Nature Center Patio is a covered outdoor space with wrought iron tables and chairs that can seat up to 30 guests. The courtyard is graced with a unique mural of the park created by one of the park’s volunteers.

What is included in my rental?

Whether you're hosting a baby shower, birthday party or wedding, Moccasin Lake Nature Park has a variety of options that are steeped in scenic Florida nature. You can host your event in either one or multiple areas including: the Cabin, the Meadow, the Upper Field, the Moccasin Lake Stage, the Nature Center Patio and Picnic Pavilion for an additional fee.

The cabin includes tables and chairs for up to 60 guests. Tables and chairs cannot be used for other areas.

Do you have availability?
Yes, we have availability for events in 2022 and 2023. Please call to inquire.
How much does it cost to rent?

The cabin is available for rent both during when the park is open and closed (based on staff discretion). If rented when park is closed, there is a one-time base fee of $50 and then a $50 per hourly rate. The hourly rate includes time used when setting up, caterer use and decorators.

The pavilion is available for rent only when the park is open for $20 per hour.

Tuesday-Sunday: 10 a.m.-5 p.m.

There is also 10% non-resident fee for those who live outside the city of Clearwater and 7 percent state sales tax. Adding time to your rental agreement with less than 14 days notice would be pending staff availability and would be at the overtime rate of $75 per hour if approved.

The down payment will be forfeited if for any reason the event date is canceled or changed.  If the user cancels or transfers their building rental 14 or more days prior to their event, they are entitled to a refund of any money paid over the required down payment plus tax.  If the user cancels or transfers their building rental less than 14 days prior to their event, no refund will be given.

*The city will not be responsible for items, which are lost, stolen, or damaged before, during or after your event.

When can I rent the venue?

The cabin is available for rent both during when the park is open and closed (based on staff discretion). The pavilion is available for rent only when the park is open.

Park Hours:
Tuesday-Sunday: 10 a.m.-5 p.m.
Closed: All city holidays

How close are you to the beach?
We are conveniently located in central Clearwater and are a 20-minute drive to Clearwater Beach or Tampa. We are minutes from nearby hotel accommodations, a mall and other destinations.
Do you allow outside caterers?
Yes, you can use the catering company of your choice.
Can I bring my own alcohol?

Yes, however, alcohol must be given away in an open bar format. Cash bars and cover charges are not permitted. 

Do you require us to use a specific list of vendors?
No, we do not have preferred or required vendors.
Are there any restrictions?
  • Smoking is not permitted inside of the building.
  • Users must follow the City’s noise ordinance and cannot have amplified sound outside after 11 p.m.
  • Any fabric decorations must be fire rated.
  • No tape or adhesive allowed on walls or floors.
  • No glitter, confetti or helium balloons allowed.
  • No artificial petals on ground in the building or park.
How do I reserve my date?
Complete the Recreation Facility Agreement with a staff member during business hours Monday - Friday.  Reservations require a down payment equal to the base fee (see above pricing).  Payments can be made by checks drawn on a US bank, or Visa and Master Card credit cards.  The balance is due 14 days prior to the event date.  At the time the balance is paid, you will need to fill out a diagram with your desired set up.
