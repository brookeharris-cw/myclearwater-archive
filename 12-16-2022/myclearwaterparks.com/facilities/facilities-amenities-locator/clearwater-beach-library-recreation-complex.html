  • Social Hall Rental Information

    Rent the 3,243-sq. foot social hall on the water.

  • Fees and Membership

    Recreation cards are available to residents and non-residents.

  • Newsletter

    Find the latest programs and classes at the Clearwater Beach Rec Center.

  • Parking

There are 21 parking spots designated for those who are using the library/recreation complex or those who have resident parking permits. Complex users can obtain a CBLRC hang tag permit at either the pool or recreation center during their business hours.

Amenities

  • Boat launch
  • Outdoor deck
  • Pool
  • Social hall with a kitchen
  • Three outdoor lighted tennis courts
  • Two outdoor lighted basketball courts/ three lighted pickleball courts
  • Waterfront views & park
  • Clearwater Beach Branch Library
  • Fitness Center (cardio machines, weight machines,  free weights, and 3 TVs with Cardio Theater)
  • Multi-Purpose Room
