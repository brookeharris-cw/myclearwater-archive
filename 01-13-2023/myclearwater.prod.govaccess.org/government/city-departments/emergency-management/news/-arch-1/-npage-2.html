Pinellas County Hurricane Preparedness Guide 2020

The Pinellas County Hurricane Preparedness Guide 2020 is available and includes information and tips for all-hazard planning.

The city of Clearwater is not currently affected by any storms.

  • Evacuation Zone: Know your zone, know your plan and know your role. Check your evacuation zone from Pinellas County.
  • Prepare: Review tips and planning tools in the 2019 Hurricane Preparedness Guide online at www.pinellascounty.org/emergency and prepare a stay kit/go kit and gather important papers.
  • Join E-Mail Notifications: Join by signing up for MyClearwater E-Mail Notifications or signing up for Alert Pinellas notifications
  • Social Media:
    • City of Clearwater Facebook, Twitter or Nextdoor 
    • Clearwater Fire & Rescue Facebook or Twitter
    • Clearwater Police Department Facebook, Twitter or Nextdoor
  • Sandbags: The city of Clearwater is not currently distributing sand bags
  • Special Needs: Register for special needs shelters by calling the Citizens Information Center at (727) 464-4333 or by calling Clearwater Fire & Rescue at (727) 562-4334. Click here for more information on special needs.
  • Pet Shelters: Sign up for pet shelters now by clicking here.
  • Beach Re-Entry Permit: If you own property on Clearwater Beach or Sand Key, be sure to get a beach re-entry permit before a system approaches. Don't wait until the last minute. Click here for beach re-entry permit.
  • Gas Availability: Trying to find a station with gas? Check GasBuddy.com
  • Traffic and Road Closures: For real-time traffic information, visit FL511.com
  • Mobile and Manufactured Homes: Mobile and manufactured homes residents must prepare to always evacuate when an evacuation is ordered. Mobile homes, manufactured homes and recreational vehicles are not strong enough to withstand the strong winds of a hurricane.
