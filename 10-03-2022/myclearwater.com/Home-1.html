Clearwater Nov. 8 Referendum

Published on August 17, 2022

The city of Clearwater will have a referendum question on the Nov. 8, 2022 ballot. Voter information can be found at VotePinellas.com. Polls open on Election Day, Nov. 8, at 7 a.m. and close at 7 p.m.

Post-Storm Cleanup: Residential Trash & Recycling Collection Update

Published on October 01, 2022

Clearwater Solid Waste & Recycling crews are working hard to collect Thursday and Friday service routes for residential trash and recycling pickups. Collections continue today, Saturday, Oct. 1.

Rescheduled Budget Hearing Oct. 6

Published on September 26, 2022

The final hearing adopting a millage rate and budget scheduled for Thursday, Sept. 29, 2022 for the city of Clearwater is being rescheduled due to Hurricane Ian.

A rescheduled final budget hearing will be held on Thursday, Oct. 6, 2022 at 6 p.m. at Clearwater Main Library, City Council Chambers, 100 North Osceola Avenue, Clearwater, Florida 33755

National Night Out Against Crime

The event is planned from 5 to 8 p.m. Oct. 4 at the Countryside Mall, in the southwest corner of the parking lot nearest Countryside Boulevard and U.S. 19. It’s a chance for Clearwater residents and others from Pinellas County to get to meet officers and see some of the agency’s specialty units.
05 Oct 2022

Tunnels to Towers 5K Run & Walk

October 05, 2022

Runners will honor the heroic life of Stephen Siller, a New York City firefighter who lost his life on Sept. 11, 2001 after strapping on his gear and running through the Brooklyn Battery Tunnel to the Twin Towers. The Memorial Causeway Bridge will be closed intermittently, so drivers should plan ahead for traffic delays or use an alternative route.
