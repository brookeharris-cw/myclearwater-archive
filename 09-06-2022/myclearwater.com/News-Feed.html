Published on October 01, 2022

Updated Wednesday, Oct. 12 at 8:32 a.m. Residents should expect solid waste collection delays. Each collection day seems to be taking 1.5 to 2 days to collect because of the volume of yard debris from Hurricane Ian. At the current pace, it will likely take two weeks to complete all routes and get back on a normal collection schedule.

Published on September 14, 2022

The method of water treatment used by Pinellas County Utilities and the city of Clearwater will be temporarily modified during the second of two segments of water maintenance this year.
