WELCOME TO COACHMAN PARK

Connecting Downtown Clearwater and Clearwater Harbor – come explore the new Coachman Park. The reimagined 19-acre waterfront park on the bluff is ready for you to explore its trails, plazas, and lawns overlooking Clearwater Harbor Marina and the Clearwater Memorial Causeway Bridge. Climb aboard the pirate-themed playground area or make a splash on the splash pad. Follow the beat to The Sound – the new 9,000-capacity amphitheater – for a lineup of exciting entertainment.

We invite you back to Coachman Park to discover everything Downtown Clearwater has to offer.

VISIT THE PARK

We’re conveniently located along Downtown Clearwater’s waterfront – right next the Clearwater Memorial Causeway Bridge – and across from everything Clearwater Beach has to offer. We’re about 20 miles away from downtown Tampa and downtown St. Pete, with easy access to both Tampa International Airport and St. Pete-Clearwater International Airport.
Visitor Information

GET HERE

Coachman Park is sandwiched between Drew Street and Pierce Street, just blocks away from Gulf-to-Bay Boulevard/Florida State Road 60 with easy access to U.S. Highway 19. Utilize one of the multiple PSTA routes, take the Clearwater Ferry or the Jolley Trolley, or park in one of several downtown parking lots and parking garages – all within blocks of the park.

Gather. Play. Stroll.

Coachman Park is just a brief walk away from a variety of excellent restaurants and bars, shops, and an array of arts and cultural experiences – including The Bilheimer Capitol Theatre – all found in Downtown Clearwater. Make sure to check out the Cleveland Street district for something unexpected: two city blocks have been transformed into a pedestrian oasis. Downtown Clearwater offers a walkable, urban environment that’s convenient and easy to explore.
