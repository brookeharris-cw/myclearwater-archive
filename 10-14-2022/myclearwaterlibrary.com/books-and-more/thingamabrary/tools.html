Stop Shock GFCI Outlet Circuit Tester

Advanced circuitry detects hazardous ground conditions not identified by standard outlet testers.

  • Tests standard 3-wire and GFCI protected 120V outlets
  • 3 color LED's indicate 7 common wiring conditions
DeWalt 8-pc Locking Hex Key Set

This hex key set features a locking mechanism for added torque.

  • Set includes 8 SAE hex keys in sizes 3/32 in., 7/64 in., 1/8 in., 9/64 in., 5/32 in., 3/16 in., 7/32 in. and 1/4 in.
  • Hex keys lock at 90, 135 and 180°
HDX Homeowners Tool Set, 76-pc

Set includes dozens of everyday tools to complete simple projects around the house.

  • Includes metric and SAE wrenches and sockets
  • Screwdrivers equipped with CR-V blade with magnetized head to keep screws at tip's end
  • Includes locking and standard pliers
Husky 3/8" Drive Torque Wrench

Torque wrench is made of alloy steel for strength and long-lasting performance and is ergonomically designed handle easily turns to set torque.

  • Torque range of 20-100 ft. lbs.
  • Audible click when torque setting is reached
  • Do not use with universal joints or flex sockets
Husky 28-pc Combination Wrench Set

Wrench set includes most commonly used wrench sizes for projects around the home or garage.

  • 12-point box end
  • Wrenches are 15-degree offset
  • MET and SAE sizes
Husky 34-pc Combination Wrench Set

Set includes regular and stubby length combination wrenches.

  • 9 SAE wrenches: 1/4, 5/16, 3/8, 1/2, 9/16, 5/8, 11/16, 3/4, 7/8 in.
  • 11 Metric wrenches: 5.5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, 12, 13, 15, 17, 18 mm
  • 7 SAE Stubby length wrenches: 1/4, 3/8, 7/16, 1/2, 9/16, 5/8, 3/4 in.
  • 7 Metric Stubby length wrenches: 9, 10, 11, 12, 13, 14, 15 mm
Husky 111-pc Tool Set

Tool set with an extensive assortment of tools.

  • Two 1/4 in. and 3/8 in. drive quick release ratchets
  • 14 standard length 1/4 in. drive 6-point sockets
  • 10 deep 1/4 in. drive 6-point sockets
  • 20 standard length 3/8 in. drive 6-point sockets
Husky 134-pc Tool Set

Tool set with an extensive assortment of tools.

  • Three 1/4 in, 3/8 in. and 1/2 in. drive quick release ratchets
  • 14 standard length 1/4 in. drive 6-point sockets
  • 8 standard length 1/4 in. drive 12-point sockets
Katapult Wire Stripper/Cutter

Cuts and strips 8 - 24 AWG solid and 10-22 AWG stranded wire.

  • Remove up to 1 in. L of the insulation layer (jacket layer) in a single step
  • Tension loaded wire-grip gently holds the cable while retaining cable geometry
Klein Tools Digital Multimeter, 1000V

Automatically ranging digital multimeter that measures AC/DC voltage, AC/DC current, and resistance.

  • Safety rating is CAT IV 600-Volt, CAT III 1000-Volt, class 2, double insulation.
  • Includes low battery indicator and has easily accessible battery and fuse replacement compartment.
Ryobi 4v Screwdriver Kit

This tool features a 2-speed gear box and 1/4 in. hex collet for fast and convenient bit changes.

  • Tri-beam LED light illuminates a dimly lit work space
  • Features a forward/reverse switch
  • 2-speed gearbox (200/600 RPM)
Ryobi 18v Drill/Driver & Impact Driver Kit

Use the 1/2 in. cordless drill/driver for routine drilling and screw-driving; the impact driver to turn long, large fasteners.

  • Includes 1/2 in. drill, 1/4 in. impact driver, (2) P102 18-Volt ONE+ compact lithium-ion batteries and P118 dual-chemistry charger.
  • Drill features 24-position clutch and 2-speed gear box to match your drilling and driving needs.
  • Impact driver delivers 1500 in. lbs. of torque for the most demanding applications.
Ryobi Compact Laser Level

Tool set with an extensive assortment of tools.

  • Measures up to 30 ft. with 1/8 in. accuracy
  • 14 standard length 1/4 in. drive 6-point sockets
  • 10 deep 1/4 in. drive 6-point sockets
Ryobi 68-pc Driving Kit

Kit includes bits for most driving needs.

  • Thirty-five 1 in. insert bit
  • Twenty-two 2 in. power bit, one 3.5 in. power bit
  • Six nut drivers (3/16 in., 1/4 in., 5/16 in., 3/8 in., 7/16 in. and 1/2 in.)
  • Three socket adaptors (1/4 in., 3/8 in. and 1/2 in.)
Ryobi 17-pc Titanium Drill Bit Set

Ideal accessory for drilling into metals, wood and plastics.

  • Includes 16 drill bit, and one SpeedLoad+ chuck
  • 135-degree split point drills on contact
  • SpeedLoad+ chuck accepts all 1/4 in. hex shank accessories
Zircon StudSensor HD55 1/2 inch

The Zircon StudSensor HD55 detects the edges of studs in walls up to 1-1/2 in. deep.

  • WireWarning detection indicates the presence of live, unshielded AC electrical wiring up to 2 inches deep
  • StudScan mode locates the edges of wood or metal studs up to 3/4 in.
Cell Phone/EMF Detector

Meter detects and measures two different types of fields: radiation emitted by mobile phones (RF radiation) and electromagnetic fields, or ELFs.

  • 1 meter, 1 probe, 1 user's guide/reference manual, 1 9 volt battery, 1 case.
Thermal Leak Detector

Thermal leak detector uses an infrared sensor to find leaks along wall, molding, duct-work and more. The auto color change feature provides quick feedback, showing where to caulk, insulate, etc.

  • 1 thermal leak detector (incl. 9 volt battery), 1 instruction manual.
Kill A Watt EZ Power Meter

Consumer power meter allowing the user to measure power consumption of household appliances and determine actual cost of power consumed.

  • 1 Kill A Watt EZ box, 1 operating manual, 1 case.
