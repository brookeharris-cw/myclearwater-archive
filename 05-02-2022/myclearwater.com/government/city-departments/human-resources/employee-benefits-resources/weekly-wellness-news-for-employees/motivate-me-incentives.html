The Motivate Me Incentive Deadline is Oct. 31, 2022.

Wellness Updates are on the Launchpad

City of Clearwater employees can find health and wellness updates at the city's Launchpad on the wellness page. (This link will not work for non-city employees.)

     
MotivateMe Wellness Incentives

The city and Cigna care about the health of employees and retirees and want to help them get healthy and stay healthy.

This incentive plan was created to encourage employees and retirees to participate in annual wellness screenings, preventive care visits, online health assessments, annual physicals, and health coaching. 

The program is available to all employees and retirees that are covered under the city’s 2022 medical insurance plan. MotivateMe is administered through Cigna, and employees and retirees may earn up to $200 in rewards. Please note that you must complete the GateKeeper items in order to be eligible for all other events. Starting Jan. 1, 2022, the Gatekeepers are the Wellness Screening and the Online Health Assessment. Register online at MyCigna.com or through the myCigna app, and click on the “Wellness” tab and look for “Incentive Awards.”

Contact the Human Resources Department with questions regarding the incentive program or for more information. 

Please Note: Employees who are covered as the dependent spouse of another employee should contact Human Resources to access their reward.

Police & Fire: Use Life Scan Results to Satisfy MotivateMe Screening

Police and Firefighters can use their Life Scan results to satisfy the Wellness Screening for Clearwater’s MotivateMe program.

Police officers and firefighters can download and complete this form and then have a health care professional/doctor sign it (required). These can be faxed or electronically uploaded to MyCigna.com.

Note that Life Scan satisfies the Wellness Screening which must be completed in order to earn any incentives. An additional Annual Physical can also be completed for the $50 reward.

  • Fax: (877) 916-5406 (Enter “CONFIDENTIAL” on the fax cover sheet)
  • Online: Electronically upload your form at MyCigna.com

Questions About Wellness?

If you have questions about the city's wellness offerings or health and lifestyle information, reach out to Leo Garrison, the city's wellness coordinator.
