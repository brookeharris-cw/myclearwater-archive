Doing Business In Clearwater

At the city of Clearwater, we know the success of your business is crucial to our success as a city. Whether you own an established Clearwater business, want to start a new business, or want to move your business to Clearwater, the city of Clearwater is here to help you every step of the way!

We invite you to explore our recently enhanced web tools that now include demographic, workforce, education, zoning and property data.

 

We Are Here To Help

You will find assistance through the city's Planning & Development Department, the Economic Development & Housing Department, and other departments. The city offers incentives to new or expanding Clearwater businesses to help them become and remain globally competitive.

Access Clearwater's Community Development Code or our Code of Ordinances. You can also learn more about the city's Community Redevelopment Agency (CRA).

We want to make sure your business in Clearwater succeeds!
