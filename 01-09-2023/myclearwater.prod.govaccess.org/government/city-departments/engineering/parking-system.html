North Beach Parking Plaza is No Longer Owned & Operated by City

The city has received complaints about the sudden increase in parking rates at the North Beach Parking Plaza parking garage. As of May 6, 2022, the North Beach Parking Plaza is now privately owned, and the new owners control the rates.

In June 2021, the Clearwater City Council declared the city’s stake in the North Beach Parking Plaza to be surplus, for the purpose of sale. This property was then advertised for sale by public bid and awarded to the highest bidder. Legal challenges were raised that delayed the closing, but once these were resolved, the sale was finalized May 6, 2022.  

The difficult decision to sell was discussed at public meetings, where it was determined that the high operating expenses, compared to the low revenue the city received from our spaces, was not financially sustainable.  The city wishes the new owners the best of luck with the continued operation of the North Beach Parking Plaza.

Whether you're coming to Clearwater Beach to stick your toes in the sand or to Downtown Clearwater for a show or dining experience, there are many parking options to get to your destination and on your way to having fun.
