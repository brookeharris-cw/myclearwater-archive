Nutrition Seminar & Workshop

July is a focus on Nutrition Education.

We have two engaging educational offerings this month on nutrition training.

Join in for an educational seminar with BayCare, "Exploring Nutrition Myths." We will focus on nutrition myths and explore evidence-based research. This virtual discussion will be hosted by registered dietitians.

  • Thursday, July 14
  • 11 to 11:45 a.m.
  • Register here 

Wednesday Wellness Workshop – "Eat This, Not That!"

Attend in-person or via Teams for this fun, interactive 30-minute workshop. We will play 20 questions, learn clean vs. unclean nutrition tips, and have prize drawings for attendees.

  • Wednesday, July 20
  • Noon to 12:30 p.m.
  • MSB Room 130
  • Taught by Leo Garrison, Cigna wellbeing coordinator
What’s Your Wellness?

We want to hear from you about your personal wellness goals and successes.

Send us a few sentences (and a selfie, if you like) on what aspects of wellness you are involved in. We would like to begin an employee highlight and share what other city employees are doing for their wellness journey. Send the email to Leo.Garrison@MyClearwater.com.

This Week's Employee Highlight

Matt Jackson, Assistant Director | Community Redevelopment Agency: 

  • “After joining the CRA, I quickly learned employees were not aware that they can receive a city recreation center recreation card for free. A few employees took advantage of the program, and we work out together multiple times each week during lunch breaks. We now are accountable to each other to work out and spend time out of the office, which benefits our physical and mental wellness.”

Send us an answer to the question, “What’s your wellness?”

Active & Fit is Waiving Their $25 Enrollment Fee Now Thru Sept. 30

In addition to their network of 11,500+ fitness centers, Active & Fit Direct has expanded its on-demand content to include 6,500+ on-demand workout videos at no additional cost.

The monthly membership cost starts at $25/month.

To enroll in the Active & Fit program, sign into your MyCigna.com account. If you don’t have an account you will need to create one. Once you login in hover over the ‘Wellness’ tab on the top of the homepage, click on ‘Exercise’, scroll down to ‘Healthy Rewards’ and in the ‘Gym Memberships & Digital Workouts’ box, click on ‘Start Savings’. At checkout, use the customer promo code, SUMMERSAVINGS. See attached flyer for more information!

Financial Webinars

Financial Planning Basics: Do You Have a Tailored Financial Plan in Place?

Wednesday, August 10, 2022 at 1 p.m. ET

Everyone can benefit from a financial plan that is tailored to individual needs and circumstances. A financial roadmap can motivate you to save money, help you meet your financial goals, and improve your overall financial security now and in the future.

Will You Have Enough to Retire Comfortably?

Wednesday, August 24, 2022 at 1 p.m. ET

Most of us imagine retirement as a happy time, a reward for a lifetime of hard work, full of possibility and potential. We have good reason to see retirement in a positive light. Americans are living longer, healthier lives than ever before. In fact, for some of us, retirement will make up a full third of our lives. Of course, this means that our retirement assets will have to do more for us over a longer period of time. This makes planning for our retirement essential.

For additional upcoming webinars, visit mission-sq.org/cfpwebinars2022.
