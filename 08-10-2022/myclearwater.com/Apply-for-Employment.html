Apply for Employment

Are you interested in working for the bright and beautiful city of Clearwater?

Browse for open positions and apply online.

Apply Online

Step 1.Browse jobs

We post jobs on our job portal at GovernmentJobs.com. 
Browse through current job openings or use the search bar.
You can also sort and filter jobs.
Click the job title for a detailed job description.

View Job Listings

Step 2.Gather your information

You may need the following documents for your job application:

  • Driver’s License
  • Email Address
  • Cover Letter
  • Resume
  • References
  • Certifications
  • License(s)
  • Transcripts

Step 3.Apply for the job(s) you're interested in

We only accept applications submitted through our online job portal. When you find the job you want to apply for, click the title and then the green Apply button at the top right corner.

Apply for Jobs

Step 4.We will review your application

Apply In Person

Step 1.Applications are accepted ONLINE ONLY.

Printed applications will not be accepted.
