Registration & Forms 

To register a new team please email athletics@myclearwater.com or call 727-562-4802 to sign up before the end of the registration period. 

To register an existing team please complete the online registration form by clicking on the registration button during registration dates. A member of the Athletics staff will follow up to finalize your registration.

For more information about rules and regulations, please email athletics@myclearwater.com or call (727) 562-4802.
