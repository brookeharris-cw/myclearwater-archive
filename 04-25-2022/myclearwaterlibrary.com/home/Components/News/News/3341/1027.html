Donate pet supplies at your nearest Clearwater library

Post Date:05/24/2021 11:52 AM
Now through August 13th, "be cool" and help our furry friends in need: donate pet supplies at your nearest #ClearwaterLibrary! Donations will benefit animals being cared for by the #SuncoastAnimalLeague.
 
Suncoast Animal League Wish List:
  1. Authority Dry Food: Cat, Kitten, Dog, Puppy
  2. Royal Canin Mother & Baby Cat Food (Wet & Dry)
  3. Folders: 2 Pockets
  4. Paper Towels
  5. Arm & Hammer Dust-Free Cat Litter
  6. Esbilac Puppy Replacement Formula (Dry)
  7. KMR Kitten Replacement Formula (Dry)
  8. Dog Crates: M, L, XL
  9. 6-foot Dog Leashes (Non-Retractable)
  10. Dog Collars: S, M, L
  11. Nylabones: M, L
Stamps & Monetary Donations of any kind (including gift cards) should be sent directly to the Suncoast Animal League. You may also donate directly online at the Suncoast Animal League's website.
