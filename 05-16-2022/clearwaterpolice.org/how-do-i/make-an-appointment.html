How Do I Make An Appointment?

The lobby of the Clearwater Police Department is open by appointment only for limited services. Appointments are first-come, first-served, and will be managed to allow for social distancing between customers. To make an appointment, click the appropriate service:

Barrier Island Re-Entry Pass

Fingerprinting

Trespass Signage

Many other services can be handled online or by phone.

  • Online: www.clearwaterpolice.org
  • Phone: 727-562-4441 or 727-562-4420

  • Barrier Island Re-Entry Pass

    The Barrier Island Re-Entry Program was established to keep beach areas safe in the aftermath of a hurricane.

  • Trespass Signage

    A citizen does not qualify for a blanket trespass sign. A blanket trespass sign is only available to businesses.

  • Fingerprinting

    The fingerprinting services offered by the Clearwater Police Department are limited for specific purposes.
